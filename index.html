A LITTLE ABOUT US

Inspiration can be found anywhere

Our mission is to join the passion for art and the beauty of the original italian leather bags together. We consider ourself an "art company" and not just a new leather bag company. A painted bag becomes a real piece of art, with real feelings and passion. Beside the paintings, we also provide also execellent solid italian leather bags made in Florence, Italy.

The artist

Kristine is a classically trained painter and successful fashion designer living in Florence Italy. She decided to put her love of art and fashion together and Boheme Antik was born. All painted bags are personally hand painted by Kristine herself. 100% Italian leather and hand crafted in Florence Italy.
